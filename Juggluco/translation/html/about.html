About

Juggluco is written by Jaap Korthals Altes

Besides the standard Android libraries, use is made of the following:

  • NFC and Bluetooth commands specific to Abbott FreeStyle Libre sensors and interpretation algorithms specific to FreeStyle Libre sensors. All are undocumented;

  • To communicate with Garmin watches use is made of Connect IQ Mobile SDK;

  • The graphs are created with NanoVG;

  • The mirror function uses Ascon authenticated encryption;

  • Communication with Libre3 sensors uses TinyCrypt;

  • The color picker is an adaptation of AmbilWarna.

Juggluco was translated into Italian by Matteo Gabriele.
