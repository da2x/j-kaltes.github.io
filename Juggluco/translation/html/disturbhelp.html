"Do not disturb" access

In Juggluco you can specify for each alarm type whether it should be played during "Do not disturb" (under Ringtone of the specific alarm.) On some devices, alarms during "Do not disturb" mode are only possible if Juggluco has "Do not disturb" access. On pressing OK you will be directed to an Android setting screen where you can give Juggluco this permission. Independent of what you answer there, you can specify in Juggluco for each alarm whether it should go of during "Do not disturb" or not.
