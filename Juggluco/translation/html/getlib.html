Libre Library

Juggluco needs a library included in Abbott's Libre app.

US Freestyle Libre 2 sensors and possibly sensors in other countries like Canada, need a library from version 2.5.2, 2.5.3 or 2.7.1 of Abbott's Librelink app.

Other sensors also work with a library from Librelink 2.4.0, 2.4.1 or 2.4,2. Versions after 2.4.2 have become worse in terms of battery usage, and give the same results in non US like Freestyle Libre 2 sensors.

The country code makes no difference.

The .apk containing Librelink is distributed by Google Play and on all kind of places it is available on the web. A list of links can be found at: https://www.juggluco.nl/Juggluco/urls.html

After you have downloaded the APK, you can let Juggluco open it, by touching the Open button.

It is also possible to run Juggluco without direct access to the Freestyle libre sensor. The app can get the data via an IP/TCP connection from a smartphone that gets the data from the sensor. Without access to the sensor, it is also possible to enter diabetes control information, like amounts of insulin, carbohydrates and activity and get this data from the Garmin watch app Kerfstok. Touch Without sensor to restrict Juggluco to this way.
