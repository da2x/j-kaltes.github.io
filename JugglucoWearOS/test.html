No WIFI

Currently Juggluco phone and watch are made to communicate via WIFI. If you are out of the house you are expected to create a portable hotspot on your phone. But the portable hotspot connection is often disrupted. The connection using the IPs connecting via the modem keep functioning probably because it is redirected to Bluetooth when the WIFI connection is turned down to use less battery power. This seems not to be the case when connected over a portable hotspot, instead of that after some time no new glucose values are send over and you have to awake the connection again.

solution

If your phone is connected via a DATA connection there is an other option on the watch4. On the phone modify the automatically created connection to "Passive only", on the watch "Active Only" and add on the watch as second ip 192.0.0.4. If your phone has a data connection and you adb to the phone and press ifconfig you should see this IP: 

v4-rmnet_data2 Link encap:UNSPEC
          inet addr:192.0.0.4  P-t-P:192.0.0.4  Mask:255.255.255.255
          UP POINTOPOINT RUNNING NOARP MULTICAST  MTU:1472  Metric:1
          RX packets:0 errors:0 dropped:0 overruns:0 frame:0
          TX packets:0 errors:0 dropped:0 overruns:0 carrier:0
          collisions:0 txqueuelen:500
          RX bytes:0 TX bytes:0

This way you can also turn off WIFI on your watch, while it is turned on on your phone and still receive reliably glucose values from the watch to the phone (or the other way around). Later I will try to automate this.
