Without NFC

To scan a Freestyle Libre 2 sensor, you need NFC and without first scanning you don't get a Bluetooth connection with a sensor. But there are ways to transfer information received by NFC to a phone without NFC. Juggluco can use it's mirror function for this. A device that receives scan and stream data from a phone with NFC also receives the information to connect with the sensor via Bluetooth. After it has received this information you only need remove this connection (or turn it the other way around) and turn on "Sensor via Bluetooth" and enable Bluetooth and turn off Bluetooth in the phone that previously had Bluetooth contact with the sensor until a connection is established.

Also the patched version of the US Libre 2.71 Android app, can be installed on a phone without NFC. To make it connect with the sensor via Bluetooth you have to copy the information from another phone with NFC that has gained contact with the sensor via NFC.

You have first to enable developer options on your phone, enable USB debugging in developer options and install adb on your computer

Install the patched US libre 2 on both phones and page through the setup until the app is ready for scanning. Scan the patched Libre 2 app on the phone with NFC a few times. Then kill the app on both phones.

Connect the phone with NFC to the computer via USB to the apps data directory:

On Computer:

adb shell run-as test.freestylelibre2.us tar -cf  -  .  > all.tar

Disconnect the phone with NFC and connect the phone without NFC. Login to Phone without NFC:

adb shell
run-as test.freestylelibre2.us
rm -rf *
exit
exit

Back on Computer:

cat all.tar | adb shell run-as test.freestylelibre2.us tar -xf -

This will give a lot of tar: chown 10379:10379 '.': Operation not permitted errors, but that is no problem. Now turn off Bluetooth in the phone with NFC and turn on bluetooth in the phone without NFC and start the patched Libre 2 app on the phone without NFC and wait for the establishment of a Bluetooth connection and setup Juggluco to receive glucose values from the patched US Libre 2 app.
